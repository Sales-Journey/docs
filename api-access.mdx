## Requesting access

To request access to API Key and secret, your must have a Sales Journey account
enabled with a valid plan.

<Steps>
  <Step title="First Step">
    Access Sales Journey: app.salesjourney.io with a valid account.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Second Step">
    On the top menu, click in "Settings" <Icon icon="gear" size={16} />

  </Step>
  <Step title="Third Step">
    Click in "Integrations".
  </Step>
</Steps>
